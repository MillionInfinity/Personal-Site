Bookalander

a Friendly and Easy Use Book Application.

Overview

Boocalander is a friendly and easy to use single page app, designed to help users sort out which books they have checked out, Along with their corresponding over their due dates. Users also can create, add, edit and delete from a profile.

Tools

Vanilia js , bootstrap, grunt, npm , jquery, sass, Firebase

Timeline

March 15, 2018 -March 20, 2018

Link

Initial Concept

Challenge Statement

"How might... an app help users track the books that they have borrowed in the best easiest way possible."

Insight

Based on my previous experience I have observed my nieces’ and nephew struggling to keep track of their personal books and borrowed books

User Research

Interviews were conducted on four people (Father, Mother, two Children). The interview questions were designed to gather information about the interviewees' experience as to how they organize the books they borrowed from the library.
